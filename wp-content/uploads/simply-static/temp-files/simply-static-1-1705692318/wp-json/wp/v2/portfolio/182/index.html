  1. Determine the Workshop Focus: Decide on the specific type of resin workshop you want to offer. It could be an introductory workshop for beginners, a workshop focused on a particular resin technique (e.g., resin pouring, resin casting, resin jewelry making), or an advanced workshop for experienced resin artists.<\/li>\n\n\n\n

This may include epoxy resin, silicone molds, mixing cups and sticks, pigments or dyes, protective gloves and aprons, heat guns or torches for removing bubbles, and a well-ventilated workspace<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n\n\n\n
